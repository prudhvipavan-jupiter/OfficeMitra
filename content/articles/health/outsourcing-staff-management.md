## Overview

Guidelines for managing outsourced and contract staff in AP Health Department institutions — agency agreements, attendance, billing, compliance, and coordination with establishment section.

DH Kurnool outsourced housekeeping to M/s. CleanCare Agency (25 staff) vide DMHO agreement dated 01-04-2025. Smt. Lakshmi maintained outsourced register and verified monthly attendance sheets.

## Applicable Rules

- G.O.Ms. on outsourcing of non-clinical services in Health Department
- AP Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act compliance
- Minimum Wages Act applicability to outsourced staff
- Health Department outsourcing policy circulars
- EHS (Employees Health Scheme) exclusions for contract staff

## Government Orders

Health Department outsourcing G.O.Ms. on [GOIR](https://goir.ap.gov.in/) and [health.ap.gov.in](https://health.ap.gov.in/). Contract labour compliance on AP Labour Department portal.

## Procedure

1. Verify outsourcing agency agreement/MoU is valid and approved by competent authority
2. Maintain outsourced staff register with agency, role, and deployment date
3. Monitor daily attendance through agency supervisor and hospital records
4. Verify agency invoices against deployed strength and attendance
5. Ensure agency compliance — EPF, ESIC, minimum wages, ID cards
6. Process agency bills through contingent bill with attendance annexure
7. Conduct periodic review of agency performance and staff adequacy
8. Report non-compliance or staffing gaps to DMHO/Commissioner

## Checklist

- [ ] Valid agency agreement/MoU on file
- [ ] Outsourced staff register maintained
- [ ] Daily attendance monitored and recorded
- [ ] Agency invoices verified against attendance
- [ ] EPF/ESIC compliance certificates collected from agency
- [ ] Minimum wage payment verified
- [ ] Agency bills processed with attendance annexure
- [ ] Periodic performance review conducted

## Practical Example

DH Kurnool outsourced housekeeping to M/s. CleanCare Agency (25 staff) vide DMHO agreement dated 01-04-2025. Smt. Lakshmi maintained outsourced register and verified monthly attendance sheets.

January 2026 invoice: Rs. 3,75,000 for 25 staff × Rs. 15,000. Attendance verified — 23 staff present daily average. Bill processed as CB-270/2025-26 with attendance annexure.

## Service Register Entry

Not applicable — outsourced staff are not government employees with SR.

## Common Audit Objections

1. Outsourcing without valid agency agreement
2. Payment for staff not actually deployed
3. Agency non-compliance with EPF/ESIC not monitored
4. Bills processed without attendance verification
5. Outsourced staff performing core government functions without approval
