## Overview

Complete guide to APGLI loan application, eligibility verification, sanction process, disbursement, and recovery through monthly pay bills for Health Department employees.

Smt. Lakshmi, Staff Nurse, applied for APGLI loan of Rs. 2,00,000 against Policy No. 7654321 (SA Rs. 10,00,000, 8 years subscription). Smt. Parvathi, establishment clerk, verified eligibility and forwarded through DDO.

## Government Orders

APGLI loan rules and application forms on [apgli.ap.gov.in](https://www.apgli.ap.gov.in/). Recovery instructions on [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/) and CFMS.

## Procedure

1. Verify employee has active APGLI policy with minimum subscription period
2. Check loan eligibility — surrender value, premium payment history, existing loans
3. Collect loan application with policy number and purpose declaration
4. Forward application through DDO to APGLI Divisional Office with NOC
5. Track sanction from APGLI Department with sanctioned amount and EMI
6. After disbursement, enter loan recovery in monthly pay bill through CFMS
7. Maintain APGLI loan register with principal, interest, and balance
8. Issue clearance certificate after full repayment for next loan eligibility

## Checklist

- [ ] Active APGLI policy verified
- [ ] Minimum subscription period completed
- [ ] No existing loan default or overdue
- [ ] Loan amount within eligible ceiling
- [ ] Application complete with DDO forwarding
- [ ] Sanction order received from APGLI
- [ ] Recovery entered in pay bill from disbursement month
- [ ] Loan register maintained with running balance

## Practical Example

Smt. Lakshmi, Staff Nurse, applied for APGLI loan of Rs. 2,00,000 against Policy No. 7654321 (SA Rs. 10,00,000, 8 years subscription). Smt. Parvathi, establishment clerk, verified eligibility and forwarded through DDO.

APGLI Kurnool sanctioned Rs. 1,80,000 (90% of surrender value) with EMI Rs. 8,500 for 24 months. Recovery started in CFMS from February 2026 pay bill.

## Common Audit Objections

1. Loan disbursed without proper APGLI sanction
2. Recovery not started in pay bill after disbursement
3. Loan amount exceeding eligible ceiling
4. Recovery stopped before full repayment without clearance
5. Loan granted with premium defaults
