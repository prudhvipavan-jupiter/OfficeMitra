## Overview

Common APGLI processing mistakes in Health Department offices — wrong premium deduction, missing registration, loan recovery errors — and their corrective solutions.

During monthly check, Smt. Lakshmi found Sri Rama Rao's APGLI premium was deducted at Rs. 250 instead of revised Rs. 300 since August 2025. Short deduction of Rs. 50 × 6 months = Rs. 300 arrear.

APGLI compliance guidelines on [apgli.ap.gov.in](https://www.apgli.ap.gov.in/). CFMS deduction codes on [cfms.ap.gov.in](https://cfms.ap.gov.in/). AG audit APGLI objections on [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/).

## Procedure

1. Identify common error — missing registration, wrong premium, recovery not started
2. Verify correct position from APGLI office/policy records
3. Prepare correction proposal with details of error and correct position
4. For premium short/excess deduction, prepare arrear/recovery adjustment bill
5. Forward correction to APGLI office for endorsement if policy details changed
6. Update CFMS pay bill with correct deduction head and amount
7. Update APGLI register and personal file with correction details
8. Implement preventive checks — monthly reconciliation of APGLI deductions

## Checklist

- [ ] Error identified and documented
- [ ] Correct premium/policy details verified from APGLI office
- [ ] Arrear or recovery bill prepared if needed
- [ ] APGLI office intimated of correction
- [ ] CFMS pay bill corrected
- [ ] APGLI register updated
- [ ] Monthly reconciliation process established
- [ ] Preventive checklist added to pay bill processing

## Practical Example

During monthly check, Smt. Lakshmi found Sri Rama Rao's APGLI premium was deducted at Rs. 250 instead of revised Rs. 300 since August 2025. Short deduction of Rs. 50 × 6 months = Rs. 300 arrear.

Arrear bill prepared and submitted through CFMS. APGLI register corrected. Monthly reconciliation checklist now included in pay bill verification.

## Sample Draft

OFFICE MEMORANDUM

Sub: APGLI – Correction of Premium Deduction – Sri ___ – Reg.

Error: Premium deducted at Rs. 250 instead of Rs. 300 w.e.f. 01-08-2025.
Correction: Arrear bill for Rs. 300 (6 months × Rs. 50) submitted.
Future: Rs. 300/month from January 2026 pay bill.

Sd/- DDO

## Service Register Entry

APGLI premium correction: arrear Rs. ___ recovered vide bill No. ___ dated ___.

## Common Audit Objections

1. APGLI deduction without policy registration
2. Premium not revised after increment
3. Loan recovery not effected in pay bill
4. Excess deduction without APGLI refund processing
5. No APGLI register maintained for audit
