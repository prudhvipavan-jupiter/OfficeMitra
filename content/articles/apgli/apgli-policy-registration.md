## Overview

Step-by-step process for registering APGLI policies for newly appointed AP government employees, including proposal submission, premium calculation, and DDO forwarding to APGLI Department.

Sri Rama Rao joined as Lab Technician at DH Kurnool on 01-04-2025 with basic pay Rs. 25,000. Smt. Lakshmi helped him fill APGLI proposal for sum assured Rs. 5,00,000 (premium Rs. 250/month).

## Applicable Rules

- APGLI Scheme Rules and Regulations
- APGLI Premium Tables
- Fundamental Rules on APGLI subscription
- Finance Department APGLI deduction instructions
- APGLI Department circulars on new policy registration

## Government Orders

APGLI scheme rules and premium tables on [apgli.ap.gov.in](https://www.apgli.ap.gov.in/). Finance Department deduction instructions on [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/). Registration circulars on GOIR.

## Procedure

1. Obtain APGLI proposal form from employee on joining or within prescribed period
2. Verify employee age, designation, and basic pay for premium calculation
3. Select appropriate sum assured based on pay slab and scheme tables
4. Complete proposal form with employee details, nominee, and medical declaration if required
5. Forward proposal through DDO to APGLI Divisional Office with joining report copy
6. Track policy number allotment from APGLI Department
7. Enter APGLI deduction in monthly pay bill from policy effective date
8. Maintain APGLI register with policy number, premium, and nominee details

## Checklist

- [ ] Proposal form completed with all columns filled
- [ ] Age proof and appointment order attached
- [ ] Sum assured selected as per premium tables
- [ ] Nominee details recorded with relationship
- [ ] DDO forwarded proposal to APGLI office
- [ ] Policy number received and recorded
- [ ] Pay bill deduction commenced from effective date
- [ ] APGLI register updated

## Common Audit Objections

1. APGLI deduction without valid policy number
2. Premium not deducted from date of policy registration
3. Sum assured not as per premium tables for pay slab
4. Proposal not forwarded within prescribed time of joining
5. APGLI register not maintained
