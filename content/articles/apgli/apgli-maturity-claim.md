## Overview

Process for filing APGLI maturity claims when policy matures on retirement or completion of term, including documents, DDO certification, and disbursement procedure.

Sri Venkatesh retired on 31-05-2026 with APGLI Policy No. 3456789 (SA Rs. 8,00,000, matured). Smt. Lakshmi prepared maturity claim with retirement Proc.No. 200/Estt/2026, DDO no-dues certificate, and bank passbook copy.

## Applicable Rules

- APGLI Scheme Rules — Maturity settlement
- APGLI Claim Procedure
- AP Revised Pension Rules — APGLI at retirement
- Finance Department no-dues certification for APGLI
- APGLI Department claim processing instructions

## Government Orders

Maturity claim forms and procedures on [apgli.ap.gov.in](https://www.apgli.ap.gov.in/). Retirement settlement instructions on [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/).

## Procedure

1. Identify policies maturing on employee retirement or policy term completion
2. Collect maturity claim form from APGLI office or download from portal
3. Attach retirement order, policy bond, identity proof, and bank account details
4. Obtain DDO certification confirming no outstanding APGLI loan or premium dues
5. Forward claim to APGLI Divisional Office with complete enclosures
6. Track claim processing status on APGLI portal
7. Receive maturity amount disbursement to employee bank account
8. Record settlement in APGLI register and close policy entry

## Checklist

- [ ] Maturity claim form completed
- [ ] Retirement/vacation order attached
- [ ] Policy bond and last premium receipt enclosed
- [ ] DDO no-dues certificate obtained
- [ ] Bank account details (passbook copy) attached
- [ ] No outstanding APGLI loan verified
- [ ] Claim forwarded within prescribed time
- [ ] Disbursement confirmed and recorded

## Practical Example

Sri Venkatesh retired on 31-05-2026 with APGLI Policy No. 3456789 (SA Rs. 8,00,000, matured). Smt. Lakshmi prepared maturity claim with retirement Proc.No. 200/Estt/2026, DDO no-dues certificate, and bank passbook copy.

Claim forwarded to APGLI Kurnool on 15-06-2026. Maturity amount Rs. 8,45,000 (SA + bonus) credited to Sri Venkatesh's account on 30-07-2026.

## Common Audit Objections

1. Maturity claim delayed beyond prescribed period
2. Claim submitted with outstanding loan not disclosed
3. DDO no-dues certificate not obtained
4. Premium defaults not cleared before maturity claim
5. APGLI register not updated after settlement
