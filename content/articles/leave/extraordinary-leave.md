## Overview

Procedure for sanctioning Extraordinary Leave (EOL) with or without pay when no other leave is admissible, including conditions, maximum period, and service counting implications.

Sri Kiran, Attender, DH Kurnool, exhausted all EL and HPL. He requested 90 days leave for personal reasons. EOL without pay was sanctioned vide Proc.No. 60/Leave/2025 from 01-10-2025 to 29-12-2025.

## Applicable Rules

- AP Leave Rules — Extraordinary Leave (Rules 86-88)
- AP Leave Rules — EOL without pay conditions
- AP State and Subordinate Service Rules — Counting of EOL period
- Fundamental Rules on leave without pay
- AP Leave Rules — EOL for private employment prohibition

## Government Orders

AP Leave Rules EOL provisions on [GOIR](https://goir.ap.gov.in/) and [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/). G.O.Ms. on EOL for COVID/pandemic special provisions if applicable.

## Procedure

1. Receive EOL application when employee has no EL/HPL/CCL balance
2. Verify no other leave type is admissible for the requested purpose
3. Determine if EOL with pay (rare, specific conditions) or without pay
4. Obtain competent authority approval — EOL beyond 6 months needs government sanction
5. Issue EOL sanction order specifying with/without pay and period
6. Record in leave account — note increment and service counting impact
7. Inform DDO for pay stoppage during EOL without pay
8. On return, process joining and assess probation/increment impact

## Checklist

- [ ] Other leave types verified as exhausted or inapplicable
- [ ] EOL with/without pay correctly determined
- [ ] Competent authority appropriate for EOL duration
- [ ] Sanction order specifies pay status clearly
- [ ] Leave account updated with EOL entry
- [ ] DDO informed for pay stoppage if without pay
- [ ] Increment impact assessed for EOL exceeding 6 months
- [ ] Private employment prohibition communicated to employee

## Practical Example

Sri Kiran, Attender, DH Kurnool, exhausted all EL and HPL. He requested 90 days leave for personal reasons. EOL without pay was sanctioned vide Proc.No. 60/Leave/2025 from 01-10-2025 to 29-12-2025.

Pay was stopped in CFMS from October 2025. Annual increment due in July 2026 was not affected as EOL was under 6 months. Sri Kiran rejoined on 30-12-2025.

## Common Audit Objections

1. EOL with pay granted without specific rule authority
2. Pay continued during EOL without pay period
3. EOL beyond 6 months without government sanction
4. Increment granted during EOL without pay exceeding limits
5. Employee engaged in private employment during EOL
