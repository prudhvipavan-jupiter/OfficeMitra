## Overview

How to maintain leave accounts for AP government employees — half-yearly EL credit, debit entries, leave register format, and reconciliation with pay bills for audit compliance.

During AG audit at DH Kurnool, the auditor checked Smt. Lakshmi's leave account maintenance for 10 sample employees. One discrepancy found — EL credit on 01-01-2026 was 15 days but employee Sri Rama Rao had 5 days unauthorized absence in December 2025.

## Applicable Rules

- AP Leave Rules — Leave account format
- AP Leave Rules — EL credit on 1 January and 1 July
- AP Leave Rules — Deduction for duty loss
- Fundamental Rules on attendance and leave
- AG Audit instructions on leave account verification

## Government Orders

Leave account format in AP Leave Rules on [GOIR](https://goir.ap.gov.in/). AG audit leave verification instructions on [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/).

## Procedure

1. Open leave account for each employee on joining
2. Credit EL on 1 January and 1 July — 15 days minus duty loss
3. Debit leave on sanction of each leave type with order reference
4. Maintain leave register with running balance
5. Reconcile leave account with attendance register quarterly
6. Verify pay bill reflects correct leave status (full pay/half pay/no pay)
7. At year-end, certify leave account balances
8. On transfer/retirement, close leave account and forward to new office

## Checklist

- [ ] Leave account opened for every employee
- [ ] Half-yearly EL credit posted on time
- [ ] Duty loss days deducted from EL credit
- [ ] Every leave sanction debited with order number
- [ ] Running balance correctly maintained
- [ ] Quarterly reconciliation with attendance done
- [ ] Year-end certification completed
- [ ] Leave account forwarded on transfer

## Practical Example

During AG audit at DH Kurnool, the auditor checked Smt. Lakshmi's leave account maintenance for 10 sample employees. One discrepancy found — EL credit on 01-01-2026 was 15 days but employee Sri Rama Rao had 5 days unauthorized absence in December 2025.

Corrected EL credit to 10 days (15 − 5 duty loss). Leave account corrected and certified. Audit objection closed.

## Common Audit Objections

1. EL credited without deducting duty loss
2. Leave account not maintained for employee
3. Debits not recorded for sanctioned leave
4. Balance mismatch between leave account and pay bill
5. Half-yearly credit not posted on prescribed dates
