## Overview

Guidelines for Child Care Leave (CCL) for AP government women employees for up to 730 days during entire service for care of two children, including eligibility, sanction procedure, and pay.

Smt. Lakshmi, Pharmacist, had availed 200 days CCL previously for her first child. She applied for 90 days CCL for her second child's illness from 01-04-2026. Total would be 290 days — within 730-day limit.

## Applicable Rules

- AP Leave Rules — Child Care Leave
- G.O.Ms. introducing CCL for AP government employees
- AP Leave Rules — CCL pay at 100% for first 365 days
- AP Leave Rules — CCL pay at 80% for remaining days
- Instructions on CCL for disabled children

## Government Orders

CCL G.O.Ms. on [GOIR](https://goir.ap.gov.in/). AP Leave Rules CCL amendments on [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/). Clarifications on CCL for disabled children.

## Procedure

1. Receive CCL application specifying child details and period requested
2. Verify employee has not exhausted 730 days CCL entitlement
3. Check child is below 18 years (or disabled child without age limit)
4. Verify CCL not granted for more than two children
5. Sanction CCL and note pay rate — 100% for first 365 days, 80% thereafter
6. Maintain CCL register tracking total days availed per employee
7. Inform DDO for pay adjustment at applicable rate
8. Record in leave account under separate CCL column

## Checklist

- [ ] Application with child birth certificate or age proof
- [ ] Total CCL availed + requested ≤ 730 days
- [ ] Child age verified (below 18 or disabled)
- [ ] Not exceeding two children limit
- [ ] Pay rate applied correctly (100% vs 80%)
- [ ] CCL register updated
- [ ] DDO informed for pay at correct percentage
- [ ] Sanction order issued before leave commencement

## Practical Example

Smt. Lakshmi, Pharmacist, had availed 200 days CCL previously for her first child. She applied for 90 days CCL for her second child's illness from 01-04-2026. Total would be 290 days — within 730-day limit.

CCL sanctioned at 100% pay (within first 365 days) vide Proc.No. 40/Leave/2026. CCL register updated. DDO adjusted pay at full rate for 90 days.

## Common Audit Objections

1. CCL granted beyond 730 days total entitlement
2. Full pay given after 365 days of CCL availed
3. CCL granted for third child without special authority
4. CCL not recorded in separate register
5. CCL availed without sanction order
