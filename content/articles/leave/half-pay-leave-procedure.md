## Overview

Procedure for sanctioning Half Pay Leave (HPL) for AP government employees on medical grounds, including medical certificate requirements, commutation, and debit from leave account.

Sri Rama Rao, Lab Technician, DH Kurnool, applied for HPL from 01-02-2026 citing typhoid. Medical certificate from District Hospital MO recommended 30 days rest. HPL balance was 60 days.

## Applicable Rules

- AP Leave Rules — Half Pay Leave (Rules 19-21)
- AP Leave Rules — Commutation of HPL
- Medical attendance rules for government servants
- AP Leave Rules — Combination with EL
- Fundamental Rules on pay during HPL

## Government Orders

AP Leave Rules HPL provisions on [GOIR](https://goir.ap.gov.in/). Medical attendance rules on [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/). Commutation instructions in leave rule amendments.

## Procedure

1. Receive HPL application with medical certificate from authorized medical officer
2. Verify medical certificate specifies period of incapacity and diagnosis
3. Check HPL balance in leave account (credit based on service)
4. Sanction HPL for period covered by medical certificate
5. If employee opts for commutation, verify EL balance for half the HPL period
6. Issue sanction order and debit HPL (and EL if commuted) from leave account
7. Inform DDO for half-pay adjustment in pay bill
8. On fitness certificate, close HPL and record joining

## Checklist

- [ ] Medical certificate from authorized MO/specialist attached
- [ ] Certificate specifies diagnosis and recommended rest period
- [ ] HPL balance sufficient (or commutation with EL verified)
- [ ] Sanction order issued within medical certificate period
- [ ] Leave account debited correctly (HPL and EL if commuted)
- [ ] DDO informed for half-pay in pay bill
- [ ] Fitness certificate obtained before rejoining
- [ ] Excess HPL regularized if fitness delayed

## Practical Example

Sri Rama Rao, Lab Technician, DH Kurnool, applied for HPL from 01-02-2026 citing typhoid. Medical certificate from District Hospital MO recommended 30 days rest. HPL balance was 60 days.

He opted to commute half the HPL (15 days) into full pay using EL. Smt. Lakshmi sanctioned 30 days HPL with commutation of 15 days vide Proc.No. 12/Leave/2026. HPL debited 30 days, EL debited 15 days. Pay bill adjusted to half pay for 15 days and full pay for 15 commuted days.

## Common Audit Objections

1. HPL sanctioned without valid medical certificate
2. Commutation without sufficient EL balance
3. Full pay drawn for entire HPL period without commutation
4. HPL beyond medical certificate period without extension certificate
5. Leave account not debited for HPL period
