## Overview

Rules and procedure for commuted leave — converting Half Pay Leave into full pay by debiting twice the amount from EL balance, applicable conditions, and documentation for AP government employees.

Smt. Lakshmi applied for 20 days HPL with commutation of all 20 days (requiring 40 days EL debit). Her EL balance was 55 days. Medical certificate from gynecologist recommended 20 days rest post-surgery.

## Applicable Rules

- AP Leave Rules — Commuted Leave (Rule 20)
- AP Leave Rules — Half Pay Leave
- AP Leave Rules — Earned Leave debit for commutation
- Medical attendance rules
- AP Leave Rules — Maximum commuted leave limits

## Government Orders

AP Leave Rules Rule 20 on [GOIR](https://goir.ap.gov.in/). Clarificatory memos on commutation limits at [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/).

## Procedure

1. Employee applies for HPL with request for commutation of specified days
2. Verify medical certificate supports HPL period requested
3. Check EL balance is at least twice the commuted days requested
4. Calculate: commuted days × 2 = EL debit; commuted days = full pay period
5. Remaining HPL period (if any) at half pay without commutation
6. Issue sanction order clearly showing HPL, commuted, and EL debit
7. Update leave account and inform DDO for pay adjustment
8. Record in leave register and SR if required by departmental instructions

## Checklist

- [ ] Application specifies number of days to be commuted
- [ ] Medical certificate valid and covers requested period
- [ ] EL balance ≥ 2 × commuted days
- [ ] Sanction order shows HPL debit and EL debit separately
- [ ] Pay bill reflects full pay for commuted days, half pay for balance
- [ ] Leave account updated with all debits
- [ ] Maximum commuted leave limits not exceeded
- [ ] Commutation not granted for non-medical HPL

## Practical Example

Smt. Lakshmi applied for 20 days HPL with commutation of all 20 days (requiring 40 days EL debit). Her EL balance was 55 days. Medical certificate from gynecologist recommended 20 days rest post-surgery.

Commutation was sanctioned — 20 days HPL debited, 40 days EL debited, and full pay granted for all 20 days. Leave account balance after: HPL 40 days, EL 15 days.

## Common Audit Objections

1. EL debited less than twice the commuted days
2. Commutation granted without medical grounds
3. Full pay drawn without EL debit for commutation
4. Commuted leave beyond maximum permissible limit
5. Leave account shows inconsistent debits
