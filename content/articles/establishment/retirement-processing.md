## Overview

Complete checklist for processing retirement of AP government employees on superannuation, including no-dues certificate, GPF/APGLI settlement, leave encashment, and pension papers for Health Department staff.

Sri Venkatesh, Pharmacist, District Hospital Kurnool, born on 31-05-1966, was due to retire on superannuation on 31-05-2026. Smt. Lakshmi initiated processing in December 2025 — retirement proceedings issued in April 2026.

## Applicable Rules

- AP Revised Pension Rules, 1980
- Fundamental Rules on retirement age
- AP Leave Rules — EL encashment on retirement
- AP GPF Rules — Final settlement
- APGLI Rules — Maturity/death claim on retirement

## Government Orders

Pension rules G.O.Ms. on [GOIR](https://goir.ap.gov.in/) and [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/). GPF settlement on AG AP portal. APGLI maturity on [apgli.ap.gov.in](https://www.apgli.ap.gov.in/).

## Procedure

1. Identify employees due for retirement six months in advance from SR/birth register
2. Issue notice of impending retirement and obtain employee acknowledgment
3. Prepare retirement proceedings fixing date of retirement (afternoon of last day of month)
4. Process no-dues certificate from all sections — store, library, hostel, etc.
5. Prepare leave encashment proposal for unavailed EL
6. Forward GPF/APGLI settlement papers to concerned authorities
7. Prepare pension papers (Form 4/Form 5) and forward to AG/A.G. Pension
8. Make final SR entry, close personal file, and issue service certificate

## Checklist

- [ ] Retirement due list prepared 6 months ahead
- [ ] Retirement proceedings issued before date of retirement
- [ ] No-dues certificate from all sections obtained
- [ ] Leave account finalized and EL encashment calculated
- [ ] GPF final withdrawal papers forwarded
- [ ] APGLI maturity claim initiated
- [ ] Pension papers submitted to AG within time limit
- [ ] Service certificate and retirement order given to employee

## Practical Example

Sri Venkatesh, Pharmacist, District Hospital Kurnool, born on 31-05-1966, was due to retire on superannuation on 31-05-2026. Smt. Lakshmi initiated processing in December 2025 — retirement proceedings issued in April 2026.

EL encashment for 240 days was calculated, GPF final settlement for Rs. 8,50,000 forwarded, and pension papers submitted to AG AP by 15-06-2026. Sri Venkatesh received service certificate and no-dues clearance on his last working day.

## Common Audit Objections

1. Employee continued on duty beyond retirement date without extension orders
2. Pension papers not submitted within prescribed time
3. EL encashment calculated on incorrect balance
4. GPF settlement delayed causing employee hardship
5. No final SR entry or service certificate issued
