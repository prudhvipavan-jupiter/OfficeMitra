## Overview

Complete end-to-end promotion processing workflow for AP Health Department staff — from vacancy identification through panel preparation, proceedings, pay fixation, and record updates.

## Applicable Rules

- AP State and Subordinate Service Rules, 1996
- AP Medical and Health Department Service Rules
- AP Reservation Rules (for roster vacancies)
- Fundamental Rule 22 (Pay on promotion)
- AP Leave Rules (for eligibility during promotion period)

## Government Orders

Latest promotion-related G.O.Ms. for Medical and Health categories on [GOIR](https://goir.ap.gov.in/). Pay fixation instructions on [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/) — search FR 22 and promotion pay fixation. Reservation roster instructions issued by GAD/BC Welfare available through GOIR.

## Procedure

1. Identify vacancy and verify recruitment/promotion quota as per rules
2. Prepare eligibility list checking minimum service, qualifications, and ACR gradings
3. Prepare draft seniority list and circulate for objections if required
4. Constitute promotion panel/DPC as per rules and record proceedings
5. Prepare promotion proceedings naming selected candidates with effective date
6. Obtain approval from competent appointing authority
7. Issue promotion orders and communicate to employees and DDO
8. Effect pay fixation under FR 22, update SR, service book, and seniority register
9. Fill reservation roster point if applicable and update vacancy position

## Checklist

- [ ] Vacancy position verified against sanctioned strength and roster
- [ ] Eligibility criteria checked for all candidates
- [ ] ACRs for prescribed period obtained and graded satisfactorily
- [ ] Disciplinary/vigilance clearance obtained
- [ ] Seniority list finalized after objection period
- [ ] DPC/panel minutes recorded and signed
- [ ] Promotion orders issued by competent authority
- [ ] Pay fixation memo prepared and SR updated

## Practical Example

A Senior Staff Nurse vacancy arose at District Hospital Kurnool due to retirement of Smt. Padma. The establishment section verified the vacancy against roster point No. 45 (OC — General). Sri Rama Rao, Staff Nurse with 8 years service and ACRs graded 'Very Good' for three years, topped the seniority list.

The Departmental Promotion Committee met on 10-01-2026 and recommended his promotion. Proc.No. 15/Estt/2026 was issued promoting him to Senior Staff Nurse with effect from 01-02-2026. Pay was fixed under FR 22 option (b). SR entry and roster update were completed within 15 days.

## Common Audit Objections

1. Promotion before vacancy exists or without DPC recommendation
2. Seniority errors in eligibility list
3. ACRs not scrutinized or below minimum grading accepted
4. Reservation roster point not filled correctly
5. Pay fixation not done or SR not updated after promotion
