## Overview

Inter-district transfers require proper application, vacancy verification, and orders from the competent authority. Establishment sections must maintain a clear file noting reasons, seniority impact, and joining/reporting dates.

## Key Steps

1. Receive transfer application or transfer order from competent authority
2. Verify vacancy and category at destination
3. Issue relieving order from present station
4. Obtain joining report at new station
5. Update Service Register and service book at both offices

## Common Mistakes

- Relieving without proper orders
- Delay in SR entry at destination
- Leave account not settled before transfer

## Expert Assistance

Complex transfer cases involving promotion, leave, or pay fixation may need expert review before orders are issued.
