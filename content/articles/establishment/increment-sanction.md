## Overview

Annual increment sanction procedure for AP government employees including eligibility verification, withholding conditions, proceedings format, and pay bill entries for Health Department DDOs.

On 01-07-2025, 42 employees at District Hospital Kurnool were due for annual increment. Smt. Lakshmi prepared the due list from SR. Two employees were on EOL without pay — their increment was postponed. One employee had minor penalty with increment stoppage for one year.

## Applicable Rules

- Fundamental Rule 24 (Increment)
- Fundamental Rule 26 (Withholding of increment)
- AP Pay Revision Rules and Schedules
- AP Leave Rules (increment during leave)
- AP ACR/APAR Instructions for increment linkage

## Government Orders

FR 24 and FR 26 on [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/). Pay revision G.O.Ms. with increment schedules on GOIR. CFMS increment processing instructions on [cfms.ap.gov.in](https://cfms.ap.gov.in/).

## Procedure

1. Prepare increment due statement from Service Register for July (or prescribed month)
2. Verify employee is on duty or eligible leave on increment date
3. Check for penalty orders withholding increment or stoppage
4. Verify approved probation declared for employees in probation
5. Prepare increment sanction proceedings listing eligible employees
6. Obtain DDO/Superintendent approval and issue increment orders
7. Communicate to employees and make SR entries
8. Update pay fixation and reflect in next monthly pay bill through CFMS

## Checklist

- [ ] Increment due list prepared from SR as on increment date
- [ ] Suspension/dismissal/retirement status checked
- [ ] Penalty orders scrutinized for increment withholding
- [ ] Approved probation verified for recent appointees
- [ ] Proceedings list all employees with old and new pay
- [ ] SR entries made citing increment order number and date
- [ ] Pay bill updated in CFMS with revised pay
- [ ] Withheld increments documented with reasons

## Practical Example

On 01-07-2025, 42 employees at District Hospital Kurnool were due for annual increment. Smt. Lakshmi prepared the due list from SR. Two employees were on EOL without pay — their increment was postponed. One employee had minor penalty with increment stoppage for one year.

Proc.No. 156/Estt/2025 dated 05-07-2025 sanctioned increments for 39 employees. SR entries were made and DDO updated CFMS pay bills for July 2025.

## Common Audit Objections

1. Increment granted during suspension or dismissal period
2. Increment given before approved probation declaration
3. Withheld increment granted without penalty revocation
4. SR not updated after increment sanction
5. Pay bill not revised in CFMS after increment date
