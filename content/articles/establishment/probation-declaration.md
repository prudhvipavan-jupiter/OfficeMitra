## Overview

Probation declaration is a mandatory establishment procedure for government servants appointed on probation. After satisfactory completion of the probation period (typically two years, or as specified in the appointment order), the appointing authority declares the employee as having completed probation.

In Health Department institutions, this is processed by the establishment section of the hospital or office, with approval from the competent authority (usually the Superintendent or Head of Office).

## Applicable Rules

- Andhra Pradesh State and Subordinate Service Rules
- Relevant provisions on probation under Fundamental Rules (FR)
- Service-specific rules applicable to the category of post
- Appointment order terms regarding probation period

## Government Orders

- Refer to the latest GOs on probation rules amendment for AP state services
- Department-specific circulars on probation declaration timelines
- Verify current GO numbers with your establishment section before processing

## Procedure

1. **Verify eligibility** — Confirm that the employee has completed the full probation period stated in the appointment order (usually 24 months of satisfactory service).
2. **Obtain reports** — Collect ACR/performance reports for the probation period from the controlling officer.
3. **Prepare proceedings** — Draft probation declaration proceedings in the standard format, citing appointment order number and date.
4. **Route for approval** — Submit to Superintendent / Head of Office for approval.
5. **Issue order** — After approval, issue the probation declaration order with proper number and date.
6. **Update Service Register** — Make SR entry recording completion of probation with reference to the order.
7. **Update service book** — Ensure service book is updated and signed by the employee.

## Checklist

- [ ] Appointment order copy
- [ ] Probation period completion verified (date calculation)
- [ ] ACRs for probation period obtained
- [ ] No pending disciplinary proceedings
- [ ] Proceedings drafted in correct format
- [ ] Approval obtained from competent authority
- [ ] Order issued with number and date
- [ ] SR entry made
- [ ] Service book updated

## Sample Draft

**Proceedings of the Superintendent, [Institution Name]**

**Present:** [Name], Superintendent

**Subject:** Establishment — Declaration of Probation — Reg.

**Ref:** Appointment order No. ___, dated ___

---

Upon review of the service records and ACRs of Sri/Smt. [Name], [Designation], who was appointed on probation for a period of [X] years vide reference cited, it is found that the employee has completed the probation period satisfactorily.

Therefore, the probation of Sri/Smt. [Name] is hereby declared as completed with effect from [date].

**Superintendent**

## Service Register Entry

*"Probation declared as completed vide Proc.No. ___/___ dated ___. Appointment order No. ___ dated ___."*

## Common Audit Objections

1. **Early declaration** — Probation declared before completion of the full period. Always calculate from date of joining, accounting for EOL if any.
2. **Missing ACRs** — Proceeding issued without ACRs for the full probation period on record.
3. **No SR entry** — Order issued but SR not updated — a frequent audit finding.
4. **Wrong competent authority** — Declaration by authority not competent for the category of post.
5. **Disciplinary pendency** — Probation declared while disciplinary proceedings were pending.
