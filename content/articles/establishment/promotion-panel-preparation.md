## Overview

Procedure for preparing promotion panels and convening Departmental Promotion Committees (DPC) for AP Health Department categories, including agenda preparation, eligibility scrutiny, and minutes recording.

## Applicable Rules

- AP State and Subordinate Service Rules — DPC provisions
- AP Medical and Health Department Service Rules
- AP Reservation Rules for panel composition
- AP ACR/APAR Instructions for DPC scrutiny
- Instructions on Departmental Promotion Committee proceedings

## Government Orders

DPC instructions in AP State and Subordinate Service Rules on [GOIR](https://goir.ap.gov.in/). Health Department DPC composition G.O.Ms. Reservation roster instructions for panel vacancies.

## Procedure

1. Identify vacancies requiring DPC consideration
2. Prepare eligibility list from finalized seniority list
3. Collect ACRs for prescribed period and check minimum grading
4. Verify vigilance and disciplinary clearance for each candidate
5. Prepare DPC agenda with candidate details and vacancy position
6. Convene DPC with prescribed composition including minority member
7. Record minutes with recommendations for each vacancy
8. Process promotion proceedings based on DPC recommendations

## Checklist

- [ ] Vacancy position and roster point identified
- [ ] Finalized seniority list used for panel preparation
- [ ] ACRs for required years obtained and graded
- [ ] Vigilance clearance obtained from concerned authority
- [ ] DPC composition as per rules verified
- [ ] Agenda circulated to all DPC members before meeting
- [ ] Minutes signed by all members including minority member
- [ ] Panel valid for prescribed period noted

## Practical Example

District Hospital Kurnool had two Senior Staff Nurse vacancies. Smt. Lakshmi prepared the promotion panel with five eligible candidates from the finalized seniority list. ACRs for 2022-23 to 2024-25 were graded 'Good' or above for all candidates.

DPC met on 15-01-2026 under the chairmanship of the Superintendent. Two candidates were recommended for promotion. Minutes were recorded and signed. Promotion proceedings were issued within 15 days of DPC meeting.

## Common Audit Objections

1. DPC not constituted with prescribed composition
2. Panel prepared without finalized seniority list
3. ACRs below minimum grading considered for promotion
4. DPC minutes unsigned or missing minority member signature
5. Promotion issued beyond panel validity period
