## Overview

Zone and category promotion is processed when vacancies arise and eligible employees are considered as per seniority and rule position. The establishment section prepares the proposal, routes it through the competent authority, and updates records after approval.
## Applicable Rules

- AP State and Subordinate Service Rules
- Relevant service-specific promotion rules
- Seniority rules applicable to the category
- Reservation rules where applicable
## Government Orders

Verify latest promotion-related GOs on [GOIR](https://goir.ap.gov.in/). Cross-check department circulars for Health Department categories before finalizing proceedings.
## Procedure

1. Verify vacancy and recruitment rules
2. Prepare eligibility / seniority list
3. Draft promotion proceedings
4. Obtain approval from competent authority
5. Issue promotion orders
6. Update Service Register and service book
## Checklist

- [ ] Vacancy position verified
- [ ] Seniority list prepared
- [ ] ACRs and disciplinary status checked
- [ ] Proceedings drafted
- [ ] Orders issued
- [ ] SR entry made
## Sample Draft

**Subject:** Establishment — Promotion — Reg.

Proceedings for promotion of Sri/Smt. ___ from ___ to ___ vide vacancy No. ___ dated ___.
## Service Register Entry

*Promoted from ___ to ___ vide Proc.No. ___ dated ___.*
## Common Audit Objections

1. Promotion before vacancy exists
2. Seniority errors
3. Missing ACR scrutiny
4. SR not updated after order
