## Overview

An employee may relinquish promotion under applicable service rules. Establishment must document voluntary relinquishment clearly to avoid seniority and future promotion disputes.

## Processing Checklist

- [ ] Written request from employee on record
- [ ] Verify rule applicability for category and post
- [ ] Draft proceedings citing relevant GOs
- [ ] Head of Office approval
- [ ] SR entry noting relinquishment date and effect

## Audit Points

Auditors often check whether relinquishment was voluntary and whether vacancy was re-filled correctly. Keep complete file with employee request and approval chain.
