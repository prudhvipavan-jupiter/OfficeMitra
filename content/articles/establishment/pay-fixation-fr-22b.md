## Overview

How to effect pay fixation under Fundamental Rule 22-B when AP government employees are promoted or appointed to higher posts, including option exercise, fixation memo, and arrears calculation.

Sri Rama Rao was promoted from Staff Nurse (Rs. 30,000-70,000) to Senior Staff Nurse (Rs. 35,000-90,000) with effect from 01-02-2026. He exercised option (b) — fixation from date of next increment (01-07-2026).

## Applicable Rules

- Fundamental Rule 22 and FR 22-B (Pay fixation on promotion)
- Fundamental Rule 23 (Option for date of increment)
- AP Pay Revision Rules and Pay Scales
- AP State and Subordinate Service Rules — Promotion
- Finance Department pay fixation instructions

## Government Orders

FR 22 and FR 22-B on [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/). Pay fixation clarifications and calculator instructions in pay revision G.O.Ms. on [GOIR](https://goir.ap.gov.in/).

## Procedure

1. Issue promotion/appointment order with direction to exercise FR 22 option
2. Obtain option form from employee within prescribed period (3 months)
3. Calculate pay under option (a) — date of promotion, and option (b) — date of next increment
4. Prepare pay fixation memo showing old pay, new pay, and option chosen
5. Obtain DDO approval on fixation memo and communicate to employee
6. Make SR entry recording pay fixation details
7. Calculate arrears if any and include in pay bill
8. Submit fixation memo to AG office as per instructions

## Checklist

- [ ] Promotion order issued with FR 22 option direction
- [ ] Option form received within 3 months
- [ ] Pay calculated correctly under chosen option
- [ ] Fixation memo prepared and approved by DDO
- [ ] Employee communicated of fixation and option
- [ ] SR entry made with fixation details
- [ ] Arrears bill prepared if applicable
- [ ] Fixation memo filed in personal file and AG copy sent

## Practical Example

Sri Rama Rao was promoted from Staff Nurse (Rs. 30,000-70,000) to Senior Staff Nurse (Rs. 35,000-90,000) with effect from 01-02-2026. He exercised option (b) — fixation from date of next increment (01-07-2026).

Smt. Lakshmi calculated pay under both options, prepared fixation memo showing revised pay of Rs. 36,500 from 01-07-2026, and obtained DDO approval. Arrears from 01-02-2026 to 30-06-2026 at promotional scale were paid in March 2026 pay bill.

## Common Audit Objections

1. Pay fixation done without option form from employee
2. Wrong option applied or option exercised after 3 months without extension
3. Fixation memo not approved by DDO
4. Arrears overpaid due to incorrect fixation calculation
5. SR not updated with fixation details
