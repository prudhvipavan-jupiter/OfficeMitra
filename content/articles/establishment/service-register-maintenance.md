## Overview

The Service Register (SR) is the primary record of an employee's service history. Errors in SR entries are among the most common audit objections. Proper maintenance at the time of each event prevents costly corrections later.
## Applicable Rules

- AP Financial Code and Service Register instructions
- Departmental manuals on SR maintenance
- Treasury and establishment circulars
## Government Orders

Refer to Service Register Maintenance Manual and latest finance/establishment circulars on SR corrections.
## Procedure

1. Record every service event on date of occurrence
2. Use correct format and reference order numbers
3. For corrections, prepare note with supporting documents
4. Route correction proposal to competent authority
5. Make correction entry without overwriting — score out and authenticate
## Checklist

- [ ] Event recorded promptly
- [ ] Order number and date cited
- [ ] Signed by competent authority
- [ ] Cross-checked with service book
## Sample Draft

**Note:** Proposal for correction of SR entry of Sri/Smt. ___ at Sl.No. ___ — Reg.
## Service Register Entry

Correction entry format: score out incorrect entry, write correct entry, cite correction order number and date.
## Common Audit Objections

1. Overwriting without authentication
2. Missing entries for promotion/transfer
3. Date discrepancies with orders
4. Unsigned entries
