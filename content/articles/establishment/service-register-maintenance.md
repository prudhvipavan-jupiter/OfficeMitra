## Procedure

1. Open SR immediately upon employee joining with appointment details
2. Record every service event on date of occurrence — promotion, transfer, increment, leave
3. Use authenticated entries with order number, date, and signing authority
4. Cross-verify SR entries with service book and personal file orders
5. For corrections, score out incorrect entry without overwriting — write correction with order reference
6. Obtain competent authority authentication for all corrections
7. Conduct annual SR verification and certification
8. Produce certified extracts for audit, service verification, and retirement

## Checklist

- [ ] SR opened for every employee on joining
- [ ] All events recorded promptly with order citations
- [ ] No overwriting — corrections authenticated properly
- [ ] Annual certification completed by competent authority
- [ ] SR cross-checked with service book annually
- [ ] Certified extracts available for audit queries
- [ ] SR kept in safe custody in establishment section
- [ ] Transfer of SR when employee transferred

## Service Register Entry

SR corrected at Sl.No. ___ vide Proc.No. ___ dated ___. (Correction entries follow same authentication format.)

## Common Audit Objections

1. Overwriting without scoring out and authentication
2. Missing entries for promotion, transfer, or increment
3. Entries without order number and date citation
4. Annual certification not done
5. SR not produced for audit inspection
