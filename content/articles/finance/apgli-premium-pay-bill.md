## Overview

APGLI premium must match policy schedule and employee category. Wrong premium entries cause recovery disputes and audit objections in pay bills.

## Verification Steps

1. Download latest policy statement from APGLI portal
2. Match sum assured and premium with pay bill entry
3. Verify new subscriptions and loan recoveries separately
4. Cross-check with previous month for unexpected changes
