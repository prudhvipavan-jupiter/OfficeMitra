## Overview

After promotion, pay must be fixed in the revised scale with proper option exercise where applicable. Fixation orders should reference promotion order date and applicable pay revision rules.

## Key Documents

- Promotion order
- Option form (if applicable)
- Pay fixation order
- Arrears bill (if any)
- SR entry for fixation

Use the Pay Estimate and Increment Due calculators on OfficeMitra for preliminary figures — always verify with treasury/AG rules before issuing orders.
