## Overview

Government servants subscribed to GPF may apply for advances for specified purposes. Temporary advances are recovered in installments; non-temporary advances follow separate rules. Processing is through the DDO and AG/GPF authorities as applicable.
## Applicable Rules

- AP GPF Rules
- Purpose-wise advance limits
- Recovery schedules
## Government Orders

Refer to latest GPF rules on AG AP portal and verify purpose eligibility before forwarding.
## Procedure

1. Verify GPF account and balance
2. Complete advance application with purpose
3. Route through DDO
4. After sanction, record recovery in pay bill
5. Update GPF register
## Checklist

- [ ] Application with purpose
- [ ] Account balance verified
- [ ] Sanction order
- [ ] Recovery commenced
## Sample Draft

Application for GPF advance of Rs. ___ for the purpose of ___ — Reg.
## Service Register Entry

Record advance sanction if required by departmental instructions.
## Common Audit Objections

1. Advance for ineligible purpose
2. Recovery not effected
3. Missing sanction on file
