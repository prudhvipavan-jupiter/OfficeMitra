## Overview

Employees and dependents may claim medical reimbursement as per AP rules. Hospital establishment sections must verify bills, prescriptions, and eligibility before routing for approval and payment.
## Applicable Rules

- Medical reimbursement rules for AP government employees
- Health Department circulars
- Ceiling and eligibility conditions
## Government Orders

Verify latest medical reimbursement circulars from Finance/Health Department before processing claims.
## Procedure

1. Verify eligibility and ceiling
2. Check bills and prescriptions
3. Prepare claim in prescribed format
4. Route for approval
5. Submit to treasury for payment
## Checklist

- [ ] Prescriptions attached
- [ ] Original bills verified
- [ ] Within ceiling limits
- [ ] Approval obtained
## Sample Draft

Medical reimbursement bill of Sri/Smt. ___ for treatment during ___ — Reg.
## Service Register Entry

Generally not required unless specific departmental instruction applies.
## Common Audit Objections

1. Claims without prescriptions
2. Exceeding ceiling
3. Duplicate claims for same period
