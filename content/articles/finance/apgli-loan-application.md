## Overview

APGLI policyholders may apply for loans subject to scheme rules and premium payment history. The establishment section forwards applications through the DDO; recovery is effected through monthly pay bills after sanction.
## Applicable Rules

- APGLI Scheme rules
- Loan eligibility and ceiling limits
- Recovery through pay bill instructions
## Government Orders

Verify latest APGLI circulars on [apgli.ap.gov.in](https://www.apgli.ap.gov.in/) and scheme amendments before processing.
## Procedure

1. Verify subscription and eligibility
2. Collect application with enclosures
3. Forward to DDO for sanction
4. After sanction, arrange disbursement
5. Ensure recovery entries in pay bill
## Checklist

- [ ] Application complete
- [ ] Premium paid up to date
- [ ] Sanction obtained
- [ ] Recovery schedule in pay bill

## Service Register Entry

Not applicable unless loan affects service benefits — verify scheme rules.
## Common Audit Objections

1. Loan without proper sanction
2. Recovery not started in pay bill
3. Exceeding eligible amount
