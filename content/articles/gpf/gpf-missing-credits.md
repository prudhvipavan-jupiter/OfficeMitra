## Overview

How to identify, report, and rectify missing GPF subscription credits in employee accounts — reconciliation with pay bills, AG complaint procedure, and preventive measures.

Sri Rama Rao's GPF slip showed no credit for March-June 2025, but pay bills confirmed deduction of Rs. 3,000/month. Smt. Lakshmi collected 4 pay bill copies and submitted representation to AG AP on 10-01-2026.

## Applicable Rules

- AP General Provident Fund Rules — Subscription
- Finance Department GPF deduction through pay bill
- AG AP GPF account maintenance instructions
- CFMS GPF deduction head codes
- AG Audit objections on GPF subscription gaps

## Government Orders

GPF subscription and remittance instructions on [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/). CFMS GPF deduction procedures on [cfms.ap.gov.in](https://cfms.ap.gov.in/). AG AP GPF grievance portal.

## Procedure

1. Compare GPF slip/AG statement with pay bill deductions for discrepancies
2. Identify missing months — subscription deducted in pay bill but not credited to GPF
3. Collect pay bill copies showing GPF deduction for missing months
4. Prepare representation to AG AP/GPF section with pay bill evidence
5. Simultaneously check CFMS deduction entries for correct head and amount
6. Follow up with AG for credit adjustment in GPF account
7. Obtain revised GPF slip confirming credits posted
8. Implement monthly GPF reconciliation between pay bill and GPF register

## Checklist

- [ ] GPF slip compared with pay bill deductions
- [ ] Missing months identified with amounts
- [ ] Pay bill copies collected as evidence
- [ ] Representation submitted to AG AP
- [ ] CFMS entries verified for correct deduction
- [ ] AG credit adjustment confirmed
- [ ] Revised GPF slip obtained
- [ ] Monthly reconciliation process established

## Practical Example

Sri Rama Rao's GPF slip showed no credit for March-June 2025, but pay bills confirmed deduction of Rs. 3,000/month. Smt. Lakshmi collected 4 pay bill copies and submitted representation to AG AP on 10-01-2026.

AG credited Rs. 12,000 (4 months) to GPF account in February 2026. Revised slip obtained. Monthly reconciliation checklist added to pay bill process.

## Sample Draft

Representation to AG AP

Sub: GPF – Missing Credits – Sri ___ (GPF A/c No. ___) – Reg.

GPF credits missing for March-June 2025 despite deduction in pay bills.
Amount: Rs. 3,000 × 4 months = Rs. 12,000.

Enclosures: Pay bill copies (4 months).

Requested: Credit adjustment in GPF account.

Sd/- DDO, DH Kurnool    Date: ___

## Common Audit Objections

1. GPF deducted in pay bill but not remitted to AG
2. Missing credits not identified or reported timely
3. GPF subscription stopped without employee intimation
4. Deduction at wrong amount in CFMS
5. No reconciliation between pay bill and GPF account
