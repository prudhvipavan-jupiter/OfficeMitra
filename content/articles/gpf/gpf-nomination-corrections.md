## Overview

Procedure for GPF nomination at joining, subsequent changes, and corrections in nominee details including Form 3/Form 4 requirements and AG processing.

Smt. Lakshmi submitted GPF nomination change (Form 4) to replace her father (previous nominee) with her husband after marriage. Old Form 3 revoked, new Form 4 with husband's details and marriage certificate attached.

## Applicable Rules

- AP General Provident Fund Rules — Nomination
- GPF Form 3 (Initial nomination) and Form 4 (Revocation/change)
- GPF Rules — Nomination in favour of family members
- Succession rules for GPF when no valid nomination exists
- AG AP nomination processing instructions

## Government Orders

GPF nomination forms and rules on AG AP portal via [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/). Succession certificate requirements on GOIR.

## Procedure

1. Obtain GPF nomination (Form 3) from employee at time of GPF subscription
2. Verify nominee is family member as defined in GPF Rules
3. If employee requests change, collect Form 4 (revocation of old + new nomination)
4. For correction in nominee name/relationship, collect affidavit and proof
5. Forward nomination forms to AG AP/GPF section through DDO
6. Track acknowledgment and updated nomination record from AG
7. Maintain copy of nomination in personal file and GPF register
8. Verify nomination validity at time of final settlement or death claim

## Checklist

- [ ] Form 3 obtained at joining/GPF subscription
- [ ] Nominee is eligible family member
- [ ] Form 4 collected for any nomination change
- [ ] Supporting documents for correction attached
- [ ] Forwarded to AG AP with DDO certification
- [ ] AG acknowledgment received
- [ ] Copy maintained in personal file
- [ ] Nomination verified before final settlement

## Practical Example

Smt. Lakshmi submitted GPF nomination change (Form 4) to replace her father (previous nominee) with her husband after marriage. Old Form 3 revoked, new Form 4 with husband's details and marriage certificate attached.

Forwarded to AG AP. Updated nomination confirmed in GPF records. Copy filed in personal file.

## Common Audit Objections

1. No valid nomination on file at GPF subscription
2. Nomination change not forwarded to AG AP
3. Nominee not an eligible family member
4. Final settlement to wrong person due to outdated nomination
5. Form 4 not signed by witness as required
