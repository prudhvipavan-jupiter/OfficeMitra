## Overview

Complete process for final GPF settlement on retirement, death in service, or resignation — forms, DDO certification, AG processing, and disbursement timeline.

Sri Venkatesh retired on 31-05-2026. GPF A/c No. 12345, last subscription April 2026. No outstanding advance. Smt. Lakshmi prepared final withdrawal application with DDO certificate and retirement order.

## Applicable Rules

- AP General Provident Fund Rules — Final withdrawal
- GPF Rules — Settlement on retirement/death/resignation
- AP Revised Pension Rules — GPF at retirement
- AG AP GPF settlement processing instructions
- Finance Department no-dues for GPF settlement

## Government Orders

GPF final settlement procedures on AG AP via [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/). Retirement GPF instructions on [GOIR](https://goir.ap.gov.in/).

## Procedure

1. Trigger event — retirement, death, or resignation order received
2. Collect GPF final withdrawal application (Form applicable)
3. Prepare DDO certificate confirming last subscription and no outstanding advance
4. Attach retirement/death/resignation order and GPF account slip
5. Forward to AG AP/GPF office within prescribed time
6. Follow up on GPF statement finalization by AG
7. After AG sanction, disbursement through treasury/CFMS to nominee/employee
8. Close GPF register entry and record in SR

## Checklist

- [ ] Retirement/death/resignation order on file
- [ ] GPF final withdrawal form completed
- [ ] Last subscription month verified in pay bills
- [ ] No outstanding GPF advance/recovery confirmed
- [ ] DDO certificate attached
- [ ] Nominee details verified (for death cases)
- [ ] Submitted to AG within time limit
- [ ] Disbursement confirmed and register closed

## Practical Example

Sri Venkatesh retired on 31-05-2026. GPF A/c No. 12345, last subscription April 2026. No outstanding advance. Smt. Lakshmi prepared final withdrawal application with DDO certificate and retirement order.

Forwarded to AG AP on 20-06-2026. Final GPF balance Rs. 8,50,000 (including interest) sanctioned and credited to Sri Venkatesh's bank account on 15-08-2026.

## Common Audit Objections

1. Final settlement delayed beyond prescribed period
2. Outstanding advance not recovered before final settlement
3. Last subscription month not verified
4. Settlement to wrong nominee without succession certificate
5. GPF register not closed after settlement
