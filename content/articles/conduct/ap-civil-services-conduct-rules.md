## Overview

Overview of AP Civil Services (Conduct) Rules for government employees — key dos and don'ts, outside employment restrictions, property returns, and implications for Health Department staff.

Sri Rama Rao, Lab Technician, applied for permission to run a medical lab outside duty hours. Smt. Lakshmi checked Conduct Rule 5 — outside employment requires prior permission. Application forwarded to Superintendent with employee's declaration.

## Applicable Rules

- AP Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1964
- AP Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules
- Prevention of Corruption Act applicability
- G.O.Ms. on conduct and ethics for government servants
- Health Department code of conduct circulars

## Government Orders

AP Civil Services Conduct Rules on [GOIR](https://goir.ap.gov.in/). Property return G.O.Ms. on [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/). Vigilance commission guidelines.

## Procedure

1. Ensure all employees are aware of Conduct Rules at joining orientation
2. Monitor compliance — outside employment, gifts, property transactions
3. Process annual property returns (immovable and movable) by due date
4. Address complaints/representations about conduct violations promptly
5. If violation suspected, consult CCA Rules for disciplinary action initiation
6. Maintain conduct-related records in personal file separately
7. Issue reminders for property return submission before deadline
8. Report serious conduct violations to vigilance/competent authority

## Checklist

- [ ] Conduct Rules booklet available in establishment section
- [ ] New employees briefed on conduct rules at joining
- [ ] Annual property returns collected by January 31 each year
- [ ] Outside employment requests processed per Rule 5
- [ ] Gift declarations monitored for prohibited items
- [ ] Conduct complaints documented and routed properly
- [ ] Personal files contain property return copies
- [ ] Vigilance cases reported to competent authority

## Practical Example

Sri Rama Rao, Lab Technician, applied for permission to run a medical lab outside duty hours. Smt. Lakshmi checked Conduct Rule 5 — outside employment requires prior permission. Application forwarded to Superintendent with employee's declaration.

Superintendent denied permission citing conflict of interest with government lab duties. Order communicated and filed in personal file.

## Service Register Entry

Permission for outside employment denied vide Proc.No. ___ dated ___ under Conduct Rule 5.

## Common Audit Objections

1. Property returns not collected annually
2. Outside employment without permission
3. Conduct violation not acted upon
4. Annual property return not filed in personal file
5. Gift exceeding prescribed value accepted without declaration
