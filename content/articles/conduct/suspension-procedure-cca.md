## Overview

Procedure for suspending AP government employees under CCA Rules — grounds, competent authority, suspension order format, subsistence allowance, and review timelines.

Sri Kiran, Attender, faced criminal charges for assault. Superintendent suspended him vide Proc.No. 10/Disc/2026 dated 01-02-2026 pending inquiry.

## Applicable Rules

- AP CCA Rules — Rule 10 (Suspension)
- Fundamental Rules on subsistence allowance during suspension
- CCA Rules — Review of suspension after 90 days
- Conduct Rules — Suspension pending inquiry
- G.O.Ms. on suspension and subsistence pay rates

## Government Orders

CCA Rules Rule 10 on [GOIR](https://goir.ap.gov.in/). Subsistence allowance rates in FR and Finance G.O.Ms. on [AP Finance](https://www.apfinance.ap.gov.in/).

## Procedure

1. Determine grounds — criminal charge, preliminary inquiry, or disciplinary proceedings
2. Verify suspending authority is competent under CCA Rules for the employee's category
3. Draft suspension order citing grounds and effective date
4. Issue suspension order and communicate to employee, DDO, and establishment
5. DDO to pay subsistence allowance (50% of pay + DA) from suspension date
6. Maintain suspension register with review dates
7. Review suspension after 90 days — continue, revoke, or convert to leave
8. On conclusion of proceedings, revoke suspension and regulate pay/leave

## Checklist

- [ ] Valid grounds for suspension documented
- [ ] Competent suspending authority verified
- [ ] Suspension order issued with effective date and grounds
- [ ] Employee, DDO, and establishment informed
- [ ] Subsistence allowance at 50% pay + DA ordered in pay bill
- [ ] Suspension register entry made
- [ ] 90-day review conducted and recorded
- [ ] Revocation order issued on conclusion of proceedings

## Practical Example

Sri Kiran, Attender, faced criminal charges for assault. Superintendent suspended him vide Proc.No. 10/Disc/2026 dated 01-02-2026 pending inquiry.

DDO adjusted pay to subsistence allowance (50% of Rs. 18,000 + DA). 90-day review on 01-05-2026 — suspension continued as criminal case pending. Suspension register maintained at establishment section.

## Common Audit Objections

1. Suspension without valid grounds or competent authority
2. Full pay drawn during suspension period
3. 90-day review not conducted
4. Suspension not revoked after exoneration
5. Subsistence allowance calculated incorrectly
