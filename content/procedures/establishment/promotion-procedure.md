## Step 1: Verify Vacancy

Confirm vacancy position and eligibility list from competent authority.

## Step 2: Prepare Eligibility List

Compile seniority and eligibility as per rules.

## Step 3: Draft Proceedings

Prepare promotion proceedings citing applicable GOs.

## Step 4: Route for Approval

Submit to Superintendent or Head of Office.

## Step 5: Issue Orders and Update SR

Issue promotion order and update Service Register.
