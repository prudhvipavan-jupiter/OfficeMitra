## Step 1: Verify Eligibility

Check the appointment order for the probation period. Calculate completion date from date of joining. Account for any extraordinary leave that extends probation.

## Step 2: Collect ACRs

Obtain Annual Confidential Reports for each year of the probation period from the reporting/controlling officer. Ensure all ACRs rate the employee as satisfactory or above.

## Step 3: Check Disciplinary Record

Confirm there are no pending disciplinary proceedings or vigilance cases against the employee.

## Step 4: Draft Proceedings

Prepare proceedings in the standard establishment format. Include employee name, designation, appointment order reference, and probation period dates.

## Step 5: Obtain Approval

Route proceedings to the Superintendent or competent Head of Office for approval and signature.

## Step 6: Issue Order

Issue the probation declaration order with proper proceeding number and date. Maintain a copy in the establishment file.

## Step 7: Update Records

- Make Service Register entry
- Update service book
- File copy in personal file

## Step 8: Communicate to Employee

Provide a copy of the order to the employee and retain acknowledgment.
