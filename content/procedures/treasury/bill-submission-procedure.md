## Step 1: Prepare Contingent Bill

Complete bill with proper head of account and supporting vouchers.

## Step 2: Obtain Sanctions

Ensure administrative and financial sanctions are on record.

## Step 3: Submit to Treasury

Submit through assigned treasury with office copy retained.

## Step 4: Track Payment

Monitor treasury status and update registers on payment.
