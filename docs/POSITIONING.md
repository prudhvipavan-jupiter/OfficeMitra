# OfficeMitra Positioning and Legal Boundaries

Strategic positioning, public disclaimers, and service boundaries for V1 launch.

---

## What OfficeMitra Is

> A professional administrative knowledge, drafting, procedure, and guidance platform that helps government employees understand and process official work more efficiently.

## What OfficeMitra Is Not

| Not This | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| Official government website | Avoid confusion with AP government portals; no implied authority |
| Legal advisory service | Guidance is administrative practice, not legal opinion |
| Substitute for controlling officer | Users must follow official channels for approvals |
| Guarantee of audit clearance | Guidance reduces risk; cannot guarantee audit outcomes |

---

## Public Disclaimers

### Site-Wide Footer (every page)

> OfficeMitra is a professional administrative knowledge platform. It is **not** an official government website and does **not** provide legal advice. Content is for guidance purposes only. Always verify with current Government Orders and your controlling officer.

### About Page — Extended

OfficeMitra was created to address a practical problem: government employees spend hours searching scattered GOs, circulars, and office files, yet still lack clear guidance on how to process cases, what documents are required, and how to draft proceedings correctly.

Our content is prepared by practitioners with Health Department administrative experience in Andhra Pradesh. It reflects common office practice and is updated as rules change.

OfficeMitra does not represent the Government of Andhra Pradesh or any department. We do not issue official orders or directions. For disputed, legally complex, or policy-decision matters, consult your controlling officer or departmental legal cell.

### Content Pages (articles, procedures)

Small notice below title or in sidebar:

> Guidance only — verify with current GOs and controlling officer.

## Expert Assistance Boundaries (V1)

### Service Scope

Expert Assistance provides **administrative guidance** based on practical office experience:

- How to process a case
- Which rules and GOs apply
- Draft format and common corrections
- Document completeness checks

### Explicit Exclusions

Expert Assistance does **not**:

- Provide legal opinions or represent users in disputes
- Issue official orders, approvals, or sanctions
- Guarantee audit or vigilance clearance
- Act on behalf of any government department
- Handle matters outside Health Department institutions (V1)

### Required User Acknowledgment

Checkbox on request form (must be checked to submit):

> I understand that OfficeMitra Expert Assistance provides administrative guidance based on practical office experience. It is not legal advice and does not represent any government department. Guidance is specific to the information I provide and may not cover all aspects of my case.

### Response Disclaimer

Every expert email response includes:

> This guidance is based on administrative practice and the information you provided. It is not a legal opinion or official government direction. For disputed or legally complex matters, consult your controlling officer or legal cell.

## Brand Language

### Use

- "Guidance" not "advice" (except in disclaimers clarifying it is not legal advice)
- "Administrative knowledge platform"
- "Expert Assistance" (capitalized, branded)
- "Practical office experience"
- "Health Department institutions" (V1 scope)

### Avoid

- "Official" (unless quoting GO text)
- "Approved by government"
- "Guaranteed"
- "Legal advice" (except to disclaim it)
- Department logos or government emblems without permission
